Ingredient:

  1. Dough: Thin, Neapolitan-style crust. Simple, made with flour, water, salt, and yeast.
  2. Cheese: Fresh mozzarella, sliced or torn.
  3. Sauce: San Marzano tomatoes, often crushed or pureed. Fresh basil leaves.
  4. Toppings: Extra virgin olive oil. Optional: a sprinkle of salt and a drizzle of balsamic glaze after baking.
