Ingredient:

  1. Dough: Buttery, flaky crust that lines the deep-dish pizza pan.
  2. Cheese: Mozzarella is commonly used, sometimes in a layered fashion.
  3. Sauce: Chunky tomato sauce, often made with whole or crushed tomatoes.
  4. Toppings: Sausage is a classic Chicago deep-dish topping. Other options include green peppers, onions, and mushrooms.
