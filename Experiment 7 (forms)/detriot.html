Ingredient:

  1. Dough: Thick and airy, often with a crispy crust. Typically made with high-hydration dough.
  2. Cheese: Brick cheese is a signature choice, known for its meltability and flavor.
  3. Sauce: Tomato sauce is usually applied on top of the cheese. The sauce is often slightly sweet with a hint of herbs.
  4. Toppings: Pepperoni is a popular topping. Sometimes, other toppings like sausage or mushrooms are used.
